## 🏛️ Foundational Architecture (The 13 Main Categories)
[cite_start]This structural blueprint acts as the "Periodic Table" for the AI era [cite: 217][cite_start], strictly mapped to the original master document verified by thousands of live URLs[cite: 357].

1. **01. [cite_start]Foundation Models (FM)** [파운데이션 모델] [cite: 265]
2. **02. [cite_start]Search & Knowledge Discovery** [검색·지식 탐구] [cite: 266]
3. **03. [cite_start]Visual Art & Generative Design** [비주얼 아트·디자인] [cite: 267]
4. **04. [cite_start]Video & Motion Synthesis** [영상·모션 그래픽] [cite: 268]
5. **05. [cite_start]Audio & Music Generation** [오디오·음악] [cite: 269]
6. **06. [cite_start]Development & LCNC Builders** [노코드/로우코드 및 개발] [cite: 270]
7. **07. [cite_start]Conversational AI & Agents** [커뮤니케이션 및 챗봇] [cite: 273]
8. **08. [cite_start]Business & Productivity Automation** [업무 생산성 및 비즈니스] [cite: 271]
9. **09. [cite_start]Marketing & Sales AI** [마케팅 및 세일즈] [cite: 272]
10. **10. [cite_start]Education & Academic** [교육 및 학술 (에듀테크)] [cite: 310]
11. **11. [cite_start]High-End Vertical AI** [고급 산업 특화 (버티컬) AI] [cite: 276]
12. **12. [cite_start]Industrial AI & Robotics** [산업용 AI 및 로보틱스 (스마트팩토리)] [cite: 275]
13. **13. [cite_start]AI Infrastructure & Sovereign Cloud** [AI 인프라 및 소버린 클라우드/보안] [cite: 277]

[cite_start]*(The complete breakdown of all 69 micro-subcategories is officially mapped inside this taxonomy framework[cite: 291]. [cite_start]To explore the individual data records of vetted AI engines, please access the Live App [cite: 359] or download the official Whitepaper via Zenodo.)*
