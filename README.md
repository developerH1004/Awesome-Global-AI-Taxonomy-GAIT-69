## 📌 Abstract

In the rapidly evolving landscape of artificial intelligence, the absence of a granular, standardized classification system has led to significant market fragmentation. This repository hosts the **Global AI Index Taxonomy (GAIT 69)**, a foundational pillar for academic research, investment due diligence, and strategic corporate adoption. 

Developed through an exhaustive analysis of over **14,000 global AI services**, GAIT 69 provides a multi-dimensional framework consisting of **13 primary domains and 69 specialized subcategories**. Through a rigorous vetting process, a refined core of active, verified engines has been established and cluster-mapped into this definitive 지형도 (Ecosystem Map).
